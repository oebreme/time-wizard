How to Use

Set the Time

Use the url-paramater "?dateTime=" to set the countdown like so:
"https://time-wizard.oebreme.dev/countdown?dateTime=24.07.2024"
[this translates to]
24th July 2024, 00:00 AM

You can also define a specific time:
"https://time-wizard.oebreme.dev/countdown?dateTime=24.07.2024 06:00"
[this translates to]
24th July 2024, 06:00 AM

Custom Title

You can also specify your own title:
"https://time-wizard.oebreme.dev/countdown?title=Pomodoro"
[this translates to]

Pomodoro
